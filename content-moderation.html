How Our Moderation Workflow Operates

DPH applies a structured moderation workflow beginning with automated AI screening to detect clear violations, followed by expert human review for contextual and complex cases.

Clean content is approved for publishing, while violative content is removed and users are notified in accordance with platform policies. Severe cases are escalated to the appropriate authorities.

This hybrid approach ensures high accuracy, transparency, regulatory compliance, and a safe digital environment for users.
