Who is DPH?

Data Processing Hub (DPH) is a data technology enterprise with over 6 years of experience in Content Moderation, Trust & Safety, and AI Data Services.

At DPH, we are committed to building a safer and more responsible digital ecosystem by combining large-scale operational expertise, international BPO standards, and advanced AI-assisted moderation technologies. As digital platforms grow in complexity and volume, our role is to help organizations maintain content safety, regulatory compliance, and user trust at every touchpoint.

Our hybrid human-in-the-loop approach ensures that while AI enhances speed, scalability, and consistency, human judgment remains central in scenarios requiring nuance, empathy, and cultural understanding.

Our Vision

To become a leading global provider of Content Moderation, Trust & Safety, and AI Data Services, empowering digital platforms to operate safely, ethically, and sustainably in every market.

Our Mission

  • Protect online communities from harmful, misleading, or unsafe content.
  • Support regulatory compliance and reduce operational risk.
  • Strengthen Trust & Safety capabilities through professional workflows.
  • Leverage AI innovation while maintaining a human-centered approach.
